# Travel Documents Payment Guide for Candidates

## Overview
All candidates can now book and pay for travel documents through multiple payment methods on the bliss connect platform.

## Available Payment Methods

### 1. **M-PESA (Recommended for Kenya)**
- **Best for**: Kenyan residents
- **How to Pay**:
  - Go to the travel documents section
  - Select your document type (Passport, Medical, Invitation, Work Permit, Birth Certificate)
  - Click "Proceed to Payment"
  - Select **M-PESA** option
  - Enter the paybill details:
    - **Paybill Number**: 600100
    - **Account Number**: 0100011879308
  - Go to your M-PESA app and complete the payment
  - Paste the M-PESA confirmation SMS in the dialog
- **Processing Time**: Instant verification
- **Supported by**: ApplyPassport, Medical, Invitation, Work Permit screens

### 2. **Flutterwave / Card Payment**
- **Best for**: International candidates, card holders
- **Accepted Cards**:
  - Visa
  - MasterCard
  - American Express
- **Alternative Options**:
  - M-PESA via Flutterwave (for Kenyan users who prefer card route)
  - Bank transfers
  - Mobile money (various countries)
- **How to Pay**:
  - Select **Flutterwave / Card** option
  - Enter your email address
  - You'll be redirected to secure Flutterwave payment gateway
  - Enter card or mobile money details
  - Complete 3D Secure verification if required
- **Processing Time**: 1-2 minutes
- **Security**: Industry-standard encryption & PCI DSS compliance

### 3. **PayPal**
- **Best for**: International payments
- **How to Pay**:
  - Select **PayPal** option
  - Log in to PayPal or create instant checkout
  - Confirm payment amount
  - Return to bliss connect with confirmation
  - Paste PayPal confirmation details
- **Processing Time**: 5-10 minutes
- **Supported by**: Travel documents section

## Payment Flow

```
1. Candidate selects travel document type
   ↓
2. Fills in personal details (name, phone, country if applicable)
   ↓
3. Uploads supporting documents
   ↓
4. Reviews total amount and selects payment method
   ↓
5. Completes payment (M-PESA, Card, PayPal, etc.)
   ↓
6. Payment verified in system
   ↓
7. Booking confirmed + Reference number issued
   ↓
8. Candidate receives SMS & email with booking slip
   ↓
9. Processing begins (appointment scheduled for medical, etc.)
```

## Troubleshooting Payment Issues

### "Payment Failed" Error
1. Check internet connection
2. Ensure sufficient funds in M-PESA or bank account
3. Try alternative payment method
4. Contact support: support@blissconnect.com

### M-PESA Payment Timed Out
- Your money will be refunded to your M-PESA within 24 hours
- Try payment again with correct amount
- Ensure account number is exactly: **0100011879308**

### Card Payment Declined
- Contact your bank to ensure card is active for online transactions
- Try 3D Secure verification
- Use alternative payment method (M-PESA, PayPal)
- Contact: payments@blissconnect.com

### Haven't Received Booking Confirmation
- Check spam/promotions folder for email
- Verify phone number is correct
- Request resend from "My Bookings" page
- Contact support team

## Frequently Asked Questions

**Q: Can I pay in installments?**
A: Currently, payment must be completed in full. Contact us for special arrangements.

**Q: What if I accidentally paid twice?**
A: Contact our support team immediately. Duplicate payments are refunded within 2-3 business days.

**Q: Can I get a refund?**
A: Refunds are available within 7 days of payment if booking hasn't started processing. After processing begins, fees may apply.

**Q: Which payment method is fastest?**
A: M-PESA is fastest for Kenya. Flutterwave takes 1-2 minutes. PayPal takes 5-10 minutes.

**Q: Do I need to be a bliss connect registered user?**
A: You can book as guest, but registration allows you to track multiple bookings and receive updates.

**Q: Is there a discount for multiple documents?**
A: Yes! Booking 3+ documents together gets you a 10% discount. Contact our team for bulk booking.
